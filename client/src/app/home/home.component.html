Our Mission

Creating a space for like minded women to come together as a community to recreate, learn outdoors skills, and become better conservationists.

We provide events for women to build confidence in their outdoor skills while teaching ethical habitat and wildlife practices.

For women, by women...with help from gentlemen.

Community

Connecting like-minded women.

Conservation

Preservation today for recreation tomorrow.

Confidence

Building skills for mental health and survival.

We envision a world where women can easily buy properly fitting gear, book a guided trip, be confident in her outdoor knowledge, and have a successful experience without it being “out of the ordinary.”
